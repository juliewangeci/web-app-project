Frequently Asked Questions

Common Bakery Clarifications

Do you offer gluten-free options?
While we focus on long-fermentation wheat sourdough, we prepare dedicated gluten-friendly items every Saturday morning.
How early should I place cake pre-orders?
We ask for custom celebration cake form applications to be sent at least 7 full days before your event date.
Do you provide local delivery?
Yes, order packages exceeding 50 dollars qualify for automated drop-off delivery inside our 5-mile local radius zone.
